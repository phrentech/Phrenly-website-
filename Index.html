The clinical foundation

Three frameworks. One cohesive experience.

Most wellness apps pick one approach and bolt AI on top. Phrenly integrates all three evidence-based frameworks — seamlessly, in every conversation.

CBT

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identifies the connection between thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Phrenly uses CBT to help you spot unhelpful patterns and build more balanced thinking.

In practice: "I noticed you're using all-or-nothing language there. Let's look at what the evidence actually says..."
DBT

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

Focuses on emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness. Especially effective for intense emotions and crisis moments.

In practice: "Let's try a TIPP skill right now — Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, Progressive relaxation."
ACT

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy

Builds psychological flexibility — helping you accept difficult thoughts without letting them control your actions, and commit to what truly matters.

In practice: "Instead of fighting that thought, what if we just noticed it? You are not your anxiety."
